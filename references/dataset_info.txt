DATASET INFORMATION 

iphone_smallmatrix_labeled_8d.csv and galaxy_smallmatrix_labeled_8d.csv are 
the data matrices include the counts of relevant words (sentiment lexicons) 
for about 12,000 instances (web pages). The values in the device sentiment 
columns (last column in each matrix) represents the overall sentiment toward 
the device on a scale of 0-5. The overall sentiment value has been manually 
input by a team who read each webpage and rated the sentiment. 

The scale is as follows:

0: very negative
1: negative 
2: somewhat negative 
3: somewhat positive 
4: positive 
5: very positive
